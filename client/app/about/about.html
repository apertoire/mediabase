About

Mediabase is a proof of concept application to catalogue a media library consisting of movies, stored in hard disks on your nas, pc or other server.

It is also meant as a learning experience for some of the technologies used as the base of the application:

  • Go (server code)
  • AngularJs (client code)
  • Foundation (css framework)
  • Sqlite (backend database)
