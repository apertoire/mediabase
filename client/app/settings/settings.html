Movie naming structure

For your movie collection to be recognized, movies must adhere to the following alternative naming conventions (these examples assumed you entered "/Movies" as the media folder in the previous section) :

  • /Movies/Bluray/Avatar (2009)/BDMV/index.bdmv
  • /Movies/Bluray/Avatar (2009)/Avatar.{iso|img|nrg|mkv|avi|xvid|ts|mpg|dvr-ms|mdf|wmv}
  • /Movies/Bluray/Avatar (2009)/{VIDEO_TS|HV000i01}.IFO
  • /Movies/Avatar (2009)/BDMV/index.bdmv
  • /Movies/Avatar (2009)/Avatar.{iso|img|nrg|mkv|avi|xvid|ts|mpg|dvr-ms|mdf|wmv}
  • /Movies/Avatar (2009)/{VIDEO_TS|HV000i01}.IFO
  • /Movies/Avatar (2009).{iso|img|nrg|mkv|avi|xvid|ts|mpg|dvr-ms|mdf|wmv}

Note: The first three forms let you specify a resolution category to your movies, so you need to create these folders in your media directory. The naming is up to you and could be, for example: Bluray, Blurip, DVD, XVID and others.
