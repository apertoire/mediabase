Movie naming structure

For your movie collection to be recognized, movies must adhere to the following alternative naming conventions (these examples assumed you entered "/Movies" as the media folder in the previous section) :

  • /Movies/Bluray/Avatar (2009)/BDMV/index.bdmv
  • /Movies/Bluray/Avatar (2009)/Avatar.mkv or /Movies/Bluray/Avatar (2009)/Avatar.iso
  • /Movies/Bluray/VIDEO_TS/{VIDEO_TS|HV000i01}.IFO
  • /Movies/Avatar (2009)/BDMV/index.bdmv
  • /Movies/Avatar (2009)/Avatar.mkv or /Movies/Bluray/Avatar (2009)/Avatar.iso
  • /Movies/VIDEO_TS/{VIDEO_TS|HV000i01}.IFO
  • /Movies/Avatar (2009).mkv
